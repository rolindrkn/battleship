Instructions and Rules to play Battleship

  1. First enter your name and press enter.
  2. Place your 3 ships either horizontally or vertically in the grid.
  3. Then click on Play button and start guessing where your opponent might have hidden his or her ship.
  4. Each player can select only one block of the grid each time.
  5. The player or the computer who guesses the opponent's ship wins the game.
